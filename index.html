About FOSSMEC

FOSSMEC is the Free and Open Source Software club at Model Engineering College, dedicated to promoting the philosophy and practice of open-source software development. Founded by a group of enthusiastic students, FOSSMEC has grown into a vibrant community of developers, designers, and tech enthusiasts.

Our mission is to inspire, educate, and empower students to contribute to open-source projects and develop their technical skills through collaborative learning experiences. We believe in the power of community-driven innovation and knowledge sharing.
